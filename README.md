# Privacy Policy – Qadam App

Last updated: April 1, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Qadam is a health and fitness application that rewards users with coins for walking.

## 2. Data We Collect

**Personal Information:**
- Full name – account creation and profile display
- Phone number – login and account identification
- Age – account registration (minimum age: 10)

**Location Data:**
- Precise GPS – walking routes, distance, territory detection
- Background location – continues tracking when screen is off

**Physical Activity:**
- Step count, walking distance, walking duration

**Financial Data:**
- Coin balance and transaction history
- Withdrawal requests via Telegram bot (no card data stored)

**Device Identifiers:**
- Advertising ID – collected by Google AdMob

## 3. How We Use Your Data
- Core features: step counting, coin earning, leaderboard, territory
- Account and wallet management
- Fraud prevention and fake step detection
- Advertisements via Google AdMob

## 4. Data Sharing
We do not sell your data. Third-party services:
- Supabase – database and authentication
- Google AdMob – advertising (device identifiers only)

## 5. Security
All data is transmitted via HTTPS/TLS encryption. No card data stored.

## 6. Data Retention
Data is kept while your account is active. To delete: zerouprbx@gmail.com

## 7. Children's Privacy
Minimum age: 10 years. Parents may request data deletion via zerouprbx@gmail.com
